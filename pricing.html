Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in all plans?

All plans include access to AI-powered blueprints, live HubSpot audits, proactive recommendations, and partner briefings. The difference is in the number of projects and blueprints you can create.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing adjustments are prorated.

Do you offer a free trial?

You can generate free blueprints on our homepage to test the AI capabilities. Full project features require a paid plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and bank transfers through our secure payment processor Paystack.
