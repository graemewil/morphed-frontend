Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Morphed Intelligence?

Simply sign up with your HubSpot account, choose your plan, and our AI will immediately begin analyzing your portal to provide optimization recommendations.

What HubSpot tiers do you support?

We support all HubSpot tiers from Starter to Enterprise, with tier-specific recommendations and optimizations tailored to your available features.

Is my HubSpot data secure?

Absolutely. We use OAuth authentication, never store sensitive data permanently, and follow enterprise-grade security practices including GDPR compliance.

Do you offer implementation support?

Yes! We provide curated HubSpot partner recommendations and can connect you with certified agencies for hands-on implementation assistance.
