Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Morphed Intelligence, we collect information necessary to provide our AI-powered HubSpot optimization services:

  • HubSpot account data accessed through OAuth authentication
  • Portal performance metrics and configuration data
  • Usage analytics to improve our AI recommendations
  • Payment information processed securely through Paystack

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to provide Morphed Intelligence services:

  • Generate AI-powered portal audits and optimization recommendations
  • Create strategic blueprints tailored to your business needs
  • Provide partner recommendations based on your requirements
  • Process payments and manage your subscription

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • HubSpot data is accessed only through official OAuth protocols
  • No sensitive data is stored permanently on our servers
  • Regular security audits and compliance monitoring

4. Data Sharing

We never sell or share your data with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To process payments through our secure payment provider (Paystack)
  • With your explicit consent for partner introductions

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access and download your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke HubSpot access permissions at any time
  • Object to processing of your data

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you cancel your subscription, your data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
